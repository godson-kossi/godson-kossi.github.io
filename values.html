    Short "Story"

    During my time at Amazon, I quickly took the lead on several projects. One day, I encountered a bottleneck situation: every time an item fell into the robotic area, the entire floor had to stop while employees from the robotic zone retrieved the item before work could resume. I analyzed the problem thoroughly and proposed a solution that differed from what had been considered. I've always enjoyed exploring new avenues, so I didn't hesitate to think outside the box to find original ideas. My attention to detail allowed me to notice aspects that others had overlooked, and I suggested to my team leader that we install a rubber strip between the curtain separating our workstation from the robotic zone. This way, when a product falls, it could bounce off the strip and land in our area, preventing items from going into the robotic zone and saving time by avoiding a full floor stoppage with each incident. This experience showed me that my proactive presence and willingness to take action made a real difference in the team.
